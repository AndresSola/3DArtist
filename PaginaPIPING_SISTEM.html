Modular piping system.

Pack of more than 200 different models all textured with a single PBR 2048x2048 texture. The variations are completely compatible with each other, and they also allow subdivisions for greater detail. The set has two different textures: iron and copper, which can be combined into a final result. On the other hand, the texture is completely tileable, which allows the creation of models with unique shapes by reusing the initial texture.

I hope you enjoy the process and the finish.
